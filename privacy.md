Privacy Policy — The Flow Guardian

Last updated: September 2026

The Flow Guardian is a browser extension designed to help users reduce mental fatigue and take mindful micro-breaks.

Data Collection

The Flow Guardian does not collect, transmit, sell, or share any personal information.

Local Processing

All fatigue detection and activity analysis occurs locally on the user’s device. No data is sent to external servers.

Permissions

The extension uses browser permissions such as tabs, storage, alarms, and notifications only to:

estimate focus duration

detect usage patterns related to fatigue

trigger gentle break reminders

These permissions are not used to identify users or track personal browsing history.

Third Parties

The Flow Guardian does not use third-party analytics, tracking, or advertising services.

Data Storage

Any data stored by the extension remains local to the user’s browser and can be removed at any time by uninstalling the extension.
